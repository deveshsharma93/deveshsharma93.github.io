About Me

I am a mechanical design engineer with a background in designing, developing, prototyping and testing electro-mechanical systems in the semiconductor and aerospace industry. I have spearheaded a variety of complex projects right from the conceptualization stage all the way through to production. I also love getting my hands dirty in the machine shop and have extensive hands-on experience building functional prototypes. Having worked in start-ups, I am adept at thriving in challenging and demanding environments while approaching challenges with a can-do attitude.

Understanding a problem, brainstorming viable solutions, being passionately dispassionate to eliminate solutions to arrive at the most feasible one, and finally bringing it to reality - this process is what fuels my passion for engineering.

Outside the walls of workplace and classroom, you will find me designing and building FPV drones and robots or working on my project Jeep XJ.
