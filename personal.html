1. First-Person View (FPV) Drone




During my free time, I design, build, and fly first-person view (FPV) drones. I got into this hobby in 2012 when I strapped a network camera paired with a wireless radio onto a small R/C glider and got instantly hooked. Ever since then I have built and flown a variety of multi-rotor and fixed-wing FPV drones.

I also formed a FPV drone racing community in Bangalore (India) in 2015 and competed with the team in the Indian drone racing league (IDRL). I won the first season of the league securing 5 first place finishes out of 8 races along with my teammates securing multiple podium finishes.

Despite being an expensive hobby especially with costs piling up from crashes while racing, this hobby has taught me valuable design lessons that I have been able to apply to my work as well. After all the drone needs to surive crashes at speeds exceeding 60 mph and operate in a variety of environments.


Design Materials Used
